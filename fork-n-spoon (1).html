Where Flavor Meets Tradition

Welcome to Fork N Spoon, your neighborhood destination for authentic North Indian and Chinese cuisine in the heart of Kharghar. Since our opening, we've been committed to bringing you the finest flavors at prices that won't break the bank.

Every dish is crafted with care, using fresh ingredients and traditional recipes that have been perfected over time. From crispy appetizers to hearty main courses, we serve food that reminds you of home.

Our cozy atmosphere and attentive staff create the perfect setting for family dinners, casual lunches, and special celebrations. Come hungry, leave happy!
